What You Can Expect: Before, During, and After

We believe the best results come from understanding the process. Here’s a closer look at what you can expect during your laser hair removal journey:

Before Treatment:

  • Avoid sun exposure, tanning products, and waxing for two weeks before your session.
  • Shave the treatment area 24 hours prior.
  • Skip skincare products with retinol or acids in the treatment zone.

During Treatment:

  • You might feel a slight tingling or snapping sensation.
  • Protective eyewear is worn throughout.
  • Treatments are quick and minimally uncomfortable.

After Treatment:

Frequently Asked Questions

Is laser hair removal permanent?

It offers long-lasting results, but touch-ups may be needed over time. Most people see a significant reduction in hair growth that lasts for months or even years.

Does it hurt?

Most clients describe it as mild discomfort, like a rubber band snapping against the skin. We use cooling features and can adjust settings for sensitive areas.

How many sessions do I need?

Generally, 6 to 8 sessions are needed for the best results, spaced out over several weeks to match your hair growth cycle.

Are there any risks?

Minor redness and irritation are common but temporary. More serious side effects are rare when done by qualified professionals, which is why choosing a trusted clinic is so important.
